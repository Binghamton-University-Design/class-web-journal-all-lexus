Project 2: Working With the Past - Sketches

"Icon design is like solving a puzzle, trying to marry an image and idea that, ideally, will be easy for people to understand and remember." - Susan Kare

Working With the Past brings us back to the 80s, providing us the challenge of creating friendly, early Macintosh-style bitmap icons. Initially limited to recreating modern Photoshop icons, we were eventually given more freedom. What are some objects that are important to you? If you were on a deserted island, what would you want to bring with you?

For me, the answer was clear: a cup of iced coffee, some headphones to listen to music with, and a frying pan to make some pancakes.
